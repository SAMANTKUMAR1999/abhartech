About Us

The Company

ABHARTECH is a leading provider of enterprise business solutions (mobility, web, cloud-based) for energy utilities and infrastructure marketplaces.


ABHARTECH’s complete range of IT Products addresses the needs of both Technology and Business requirements, end-to-end. This helps organizations leverage New-Age Leading-Edge Technologies for Business Establishment, Improvement and Returns.


The expectations and demands on today’s enterprise applications are changing. They must be securely integrated in real-time with disparate data sources.


They need to exchange data with other systems on public cloud, private cloud, traditional systems and other third-party ecosystems in a world of hybrid technology. And they must be capable of running on touch devices of all shapes and sizes so that people can perform business tasks on-the-go. The way in which work gets done is changing how the business applications are developed. That’s where ABHARTECH Enterprise Solutions can contribute.
