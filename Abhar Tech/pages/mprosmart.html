mProSMART

Infrastructure Industry Globally has registered great progress in the recent years. Future growth is anticipated and expected in several areas and there has been huge proposed investments in the next five years. In order to turn the infrastructure dream into a reality, there are great challenges which needs to be tackled efficiently:

✔ Planning oriented issues

✔ Delay in implementation and execution

✔ Time and cost overrun

✔ Burden on developers because of execution delay
